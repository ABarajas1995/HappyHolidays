Happy Holidays

By Alexander Barajas

Christmas is usually a time where people come together and spend time with their families and other loved ones. There's food, gifts, music. For some people, though, it's a tougher time of the year. Growing up, Christmas was difficult and I wasn't very fond of the holiday. This is the story of how I came to enjoy Christmas. Also, feel free to shape the happy snowman's nose to your liking!
